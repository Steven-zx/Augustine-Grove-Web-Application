Augustine Grove Community

Augustine Grove stands as a distinguished residential village in the heart of Iloilo City, Western Visayas, Philippines, renowned for its commitment to providing quality and elegant living spaces. Meticulously designed for comfort, refinement, and convenience, the village offers residents an atmosphere of tranquility and exclusivity. Its thoughtfully crafted master plan integrates modern amenities with the natural beauty of its surroundings, providing access to a multi-purpose hall for events, a basketball court for recreation, and a refreshing swimming pool, fostering community engagement and well-being.

Beyond the village's serene environment, Augustine Grove's strategic location in Iloilo City offers unparalleled convenience. Its prime setting ensures that everyday necessities and leisure pursuits are within easy reach, with a diverse array of shops, reputable schools, serene churches, a wide selection of restaurants, and expansive malls all conveniently located nearby. This accessibility allows residents to seamlessly blend peaceful village life with the dynamic offerings of Iloilo City, making Augustine Grove more than just a residence—it's a lifestyle choice that values quality, elegance, and a well-connected community in a charming Philippine city.

SECURE

Augustine Grove prioritizes resident safety with a comprehensive security strategy. The village has a gated entrance, 24/7 manned guardhouse, and round-the-clock CCTV surveillance.

PEACEFUL

Augustine Grove offers a tranquil escape from city life. Designed for a serene atmosphere, the community fosters privacy, allowing residents to enjoy quiet moments.

CONVENIENT

Augustine Grove's strategic location in Iloilo City places residents near essential amenities. The village is close to shopping centers, supermarkets, and local markets, making errands easy.

House Rules and Security Regulations

Entry of Vehicles

  1. Only one side of the gate will be open, except in times of emergency.
  2. Incoming vehicles will be given priority.
  3. Drivers must observe the compulsory 20 kph speed limit that applies throughout the subdivision.
  4. During nighttime, drivers are required to switch on the vehicle passenger's light.
  5. Only those with valid car stickers will be allowed entry.
  6. For those without a valid car sticker or who have an expired sticker:
    • No ID, No Entry. The driver must surrender his/her driver's license or any valid ID.
    • The guard on duty will record the driver's name, plate number, and name of the owner.
    • The guard on duty will report to the office the list of names of the owner/driver and plate number of all vehicles that are expired and without stickers.
  7. For Visitors/Outsiders/Non-residents:
    • Vehicles will be inspected at the entry.
    • No ID, No Entry. The driver must surrender his/her driver's license or any valid ID.
    • The guard on duty will record the time in and out, the driver's name, the vehicle's plate number, the destination, and the number of passengers on board.
  8. The guard should not leave their post to call a PUV from the road, except during emergencies.

Registration

  1. Homeowners and Long-term lessees are required to register their household members and vehicles on our online database.
  2. Tenants are to be registered in the security logbook.

Vehicle and Parking

  1. Homeowner’s vehicles have to be parked properly, preferably in the garage.
  2. Vehicles parked in driveways must be parked in a manner that does not impede sidewalks or extend beyond driveways.
  3. No Double Parking at all times
  4. Street parking will be allowed only if it is on the owner's front gate/lot and does not protrude to the immediate neighbor on either side.
  5. No parking across one’s property.
  6. Commercial vehicles are not allowed to park within the subdivision for more than five (5) hours (taxis, delivery vans, service vehicles that provide utilities, etc.). They are only allowed if such is in the act of rendering service.
  7. Deliver riders, produce vendors, and PUVs are required to surrender their driver’s license or any valid ID with a picture.
  8. No parking from the gate to the entrance except for vehicles with stickers
  9. AGRVHOAI has designated parking and no parking zones. Signs and markings will be installed.

Entry of Persons

  1. Vistors of Homeowners
    • Homeowners are encouraged to register their visitors’ names, the number of their expected visitors, and the time of visit.
    • Homeowners are urged to call and register in advance at the guardhouse before a visit to avoid inconvenience to visitors, especially during a scheduled party at their residence.
  2. Laborers (plumbers, carpenters, etc.), collectors, and salesmen, among others.
    • Homeowners are requested to register the names of expected laborers, collectors, staff, and salesmen, among others, the frequency of visits, and a note to the guard on whether the visitor will be allowed entry.
    • Without the consent of the homeowners concerned, they will not be allowed entry.
  3. If no advice was given, the guard would try to contact the homeowner to confirm the visitor's entry before allowing them to enter.
  4. Scrappers are not allowed to get inside the subdivision.

Pets

  1. Pets must be inside the owner's property
  2. Pets on walks must be on a leash
  3. The owner must clean up/pick up their pet's poop (CLAYGO)/
  4. The owner must manage responsibly and prevent household pet odors.

Waste

  1. Dumping of leakage of grease and/or chemical residuals is prohibited.

Noise and Party

  1. If homeowners are having a party or celebration, noise must be minimized. Advance notice to a neighbor would be encouraged.
  2. There should be no loud noise from 10 pm to 5 am.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay my monthly HOA fees?

You can pay your monthly HOA fees via bank transfer to our official account, or drop off a check at the Admin Office.

What are the requirements to purchase a property in Augustine Grove?

Prospective buyers need a valid ID, proof of income, and must complete our HOA application form, available at the Admin Office.

How can I register my visitors for entry?

Use the “Generate QR” feature on the Security page: fill in visitor details and print or save the QR code for gate entry.

What are the construction guidelines for new homes?

All new constructions must adhere to the HOA’s architectural guidelines—see the full PDF on the “About” page or contact the Admin Office.

How can I report maintenance issues in common areas?

Navigate to the “Concerns” page, fill out the form with details and images, and submit. The maintenance team will be notified immediately.
