Augustine Grove Community

Augustine Grove stands as a distinguished residential village in the heart of Iloilo City, Western Visayas, Philippines, renowned for its commitment to providing quality and elegant living spaces. Meticulously designed for comfort, refinement, and convenience, the village offers residents an atmosphere of tranquility and exclusivity. Its thoughtfully crafted master plan integrates modern amenities with the natural beauty of its surroundings, providing access to a multi-purpose hall for events, a basketball court for recreation, and a refreshing swimming pool, fostering community engagement and well-being.

Beyond the village's serene environment, Augustine Grove's strategic location in Iloilo City offers unparalleled convenience. Its prime setting ensures that everyday necessities and leisure pursuits are within easy reach, with a diverse array of shops, reputable schools, serene churches, a wide selection of restaurants, and expansive malls all conveniently located nearby. House Rules and Security Regulations

Entry of Vehicles

  1. Only one side of the gate will be open, except in times of emergency.
  2. Incoming vehicles will be given priority.
  3. Drivers must observe the compulsory 20 kph speed limit that applies throughout the subdivision.
  4. During nighttime, drivers are required to switch on the vehicle passenger's light.
  5. Only those with valid car stickers will be allowed entry.
  6. For those without a valid car sticker or who have an expired sticker:
    • No ID, No Entry. The driver must surrender his/her driver's license or any valid ID.
    • The guard on duty will record the driver's name, plate number, and name of the owner.
    • The guard on duty will report to the office the list of names of the owner/driver and plate number of all vehicles that are expired and without stickers.
  7. For Visitors/Outsiders/Non-residents:
    • Vehicles will be inspected at the entry.
    • No ID, No Entry. The driver must surrender his/her driver's license or any valid ID.
    • The guard on duty will record the time in and out, the driver's name, the vehicle's plate number, the destination, and the number of passengers on board.
  8. The guard should not leave their post to call a PUV from the road, except during emergencies.
